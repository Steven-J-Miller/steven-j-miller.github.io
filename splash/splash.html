As the distribution of our wait times wasn't normal, I chose to use Spearman Rank correlation. I found a statistically signifcant correlation of 0.32 between the mean daily temprature and the posted wait time at Splash Mountain. This positive relationship indicates that the ride is more popular when the weather is warmer.

Hypothesis Testing¶

In [16]:
class CorrelationPermute(thinkstats2.HypothesisTest):

    def TestStatistic(self, data):
        xs, ys = data
        test_stat = abs(thinkstats2.Corr(xs, ys))
        return test_stat

    def RunModel(self):
        xs, ys = self.data
        xs = np.random.permutation(xs)
        return xs, ys
